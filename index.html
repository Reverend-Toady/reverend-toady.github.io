About

Hey! I am Aditya.
You may also know me as Toady. I started programming in March of 2020 and have since fell in love with it. I wish to pursue software engineering as a career, specifically in the backend industry.
As a hobby I love to crack jokes and have been a part of three comedy shows throughout my school life. I also enjoy making music be it via my guitar or through digital audio workstations like FL studio.
I enjoy coding in Python, the language I am most familiar with but am also expanding my grasp taking part in my project Pathfinding into Programming which aims to explore various languages and find my new favorite one.
